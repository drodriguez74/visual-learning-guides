§ 03 — Interaction Taxonomy
Layers of Fusion
L1 · INPUT
Perception
How humans signal intent: touch, voice, gaze, gesture, keystrokes. The richer the input modality, the higher the bandwidth of intention transfer.
Voice Touch Gaze Gesture
L2 · PARSE
Interpretation
Decoding raw signals into structured intent. NLP, computer vision, and context models resolve ambiguity — mapping surface behavior to underlying goals.
NLP Intent Model Context
L3 · FUSE
Synthesis
The AI merges parsed intent with knowledge, memory, and constraints to construct a response. Highest-value fusion occurs here — human + machine co-creation.
LLM RAG Reasoning
L4 · OUTPUT
Presentation
Rendering machine output in human-comprehensible form. Modality, pacing, density, and progressive disclosure are engineered for cognitive fit.
Visual Audio Haptic
L5 · ADAPT
Learning
Both human and machine update models based on outcomes. The system improves through implicit feedback (dwell time, corrections) and explicit signals (ratings, edits).
RLHF Memory Fine-tune
